This double summer school + workshop program is topically centered at the intersection of PDE and algorithms, it aims to facilitate and increase collaborations across disciplines in mathematics, computer science, and engineering. One specific aim is to bring together students and faculty interested working in scientific computing, analysis of PDE, optimization, computer graphics, machine learning, and more. The summer school (Monday - Thursday) will be held on the Texas State campus (in San Marcos), while the research workshop (Friday-Saturday-Sunday) will take place at the University of Texas campus (in Austin).
