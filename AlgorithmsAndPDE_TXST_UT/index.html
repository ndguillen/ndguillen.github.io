This is a week long program comprising a 4 day summer school and a research workshop. Topically, the week's events are centered at the intersection of PDE and algorithms. We have to bring together students and faculty with interests in scientific computing, analysis of PDE, optimization, computer graphics, machine learning, and more. The aim is to facilitate and increase collaborations across disciplines in mathematics, computer science, and engineering.

The summer school (Monday through Thursday) will be held on the Texas State campus (in San Marcos), while the research workshop (Friday-Saturday-Sunday) will take place at the University of Texas campus (in Austin).
